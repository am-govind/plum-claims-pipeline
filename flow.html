Diagram reflects the hex-layer wiring: pipeline in backend/app/application/pipeline.py, agents in backend/app/application/agents/, ports in backend/app/application/ports/, adapters (SqlAlchemy repository, JSON policy loader, Gemini/Mock providers, InMemoryEventBus + handlers) in backend/app/infrastructure/, FastAPI routers + Depends() in backend/app/interfaces/http/, and the composition root at backend/app/composition.py. Critical agents (intake, document_verification, decision_synthesizer) re-raise; non-critical agents are caught by pipeline._wrap, which records the failure on the trace, marks the run degraded, and records a ComponentDegraded event. Domain events (ClaimApproved, ClaimPartiallyApproved, ClaimRejected, ManualReviewRequired, ClaimHaltedEarly, ComponentDegraded, FraudSignalsRaised) are buffered on ClaimState and dispatched exactly once per claim, after persistence.
